a letter from chenyue.c 2026

As Chenyue turned 25 last year, she received letters from 15 cities worldwide — from friends who, in one way or another, shaped the trajectory of her life. Now, as Chenyue turns 26 this year on 6.10, she wants to write back into the world. If you would like a letter of life updates from her, leave your mailing address, and she will send you a handmade postcard. You are welcome to leave a note for what you’d like in the letter — a question, reflections on a certain topic, a memory, advice, or simply a birthday message.

In a time of disappearing distances, perhaps letters are still a way of saying: I thought of you, and wished to leave something that could be held.
